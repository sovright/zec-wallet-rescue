Why Argos

Built for one job, done right

Argos is a single-purpose recovery tool — not a full wallet. It handles the hard parts so you can move your ZEC and close this chapter.

🌱

Seed-phrase only

No wallet files, no backups, no databases. Just your 24-word BIP-39 seed phrase — everything else is derived from that.

🔍

Finds every ZEC

Scans Sapling, Orchard, and transparent pools across all your accounts. A quick transparent probe surfaces t-addr balances within seconds of starting.

🛡

Privacy first

Your seed phrase never leaves your device. All derivation and scanning happens locally — Argos connects only to public lightwalletd nodes to fetch compact block data.

⏸

Safely resumable

Long scans can be interrupted and resumed from the last saved block. Restart your computer, close the app, come back later — your progress is preserved.

📅

Birthday auto-detection

Not sure when you created your wallet? Argos can probe the blockchain to estimate your wallet birthday automatically, minimising unnecessary scan time.

🔄

Connection resilient

Automatically reconnects and retries when lightwalletd servers drop the connection — normal behaviour on long scans — so you don't have to babysit it.

How it works

Six steps from seed to safe

The whole process takes a few clicks and some patience while the blockchain scans.

1

Welcome

Open Argos and confirm you have your 24-word BIP-39 seed phrase from ZecWallet Lite.

2

Enter your seed

Paste your seed phrase. Argos validates it locally and clears it from the UI after the session starts.

3

Configure

Set your network, wallet birthday, and destination Unified Address (from ZODL (formerly Zashi), YWallet, or another modern wallet).

4

Scan

Argos syncs compact blocks and probes UTXOs. Watch funds appear in real time as each account is scanned.

5

Review the sweep

Inspect the proposed transactions — gross, fee, and net — before committing. Transparent funds are shielded first.

6

Done

Argos broadcasts the transactions and shows you the txids. Save a recovery report for your records.

🔐

Your seed phrase never leaves your computer

Argos derives all keys locally and stores a temporary wallet database only on your machine. It connects to public Zcash lightwalletd servers solely to download compact block data — the same data every full node downloads. No seed, no keys, no balances are ever transmitted. You can review every line of code on GitHub.
