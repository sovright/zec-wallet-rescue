Step 1

Recover a discontinued wallet carefully

ZECK re-derives the non-standard ZecWallet Lite address layout from a 24-word seed, then guides you through scanning and preparing a sweep to a modern Unified Address.

Seed-only recovery

You do not need a legacy wallet database to begin the recovery flow.

Local-first

Seed validation and derivation happen locally in the Tauri Rust backend.

Incremental recovery

ZECK keeps a local recovery workspace so scans can resume and real sweeps can build from authoritative wallet state.
