Important

This website is not a wallet

Never enter a seed phrase into this splash page. ZECK is a downloadable desktop app; seed validation, key derivation, wallet scanning, transaction building, and report generation happen on your machine.

Recovery Flow

Built around the legacy ZecWallet Lite layout

ZECK re-derives the address structure used by discontinued ZecWallet Lite wallets, then uses a guided scan and sweep workflow to make the recovery process auditable.

01

Validate the seed locally

Paste a 24-word phrase into the desktop app and verify the BIP-39 checksum.

02

Configure scan depth

Choose the network, birthday height, lightwalletd endpoint, and account gap limit.

03

Scan authoritative state

Use the persisted wallet workspace to scan transparent, Sapling, and Orchard funds.

04

Sweep with review

Review fees, memo, destination address, skipped accounts, and broadcast results.

Who It Helps

For owners of old ZecWallet Lite seeds

ZECK is designed for people who still have a ZecWallet Lite seed phrase but no longer have a working legacy wallet environment. It does not require a wallet database file.

Best fit

  • 24-word ZecWallet Lite recovery seed
  • Funds that may live in transparent, Sapling, or Orchard pools
  • A modern Zcash wallet with a Unified Address destination
  • Enough time to let compact-block sync complete

FAQ

Sharp edges, named clearly

Does ZECK recover Sprout funds?

No. Sprout recovery is out of scope for seed-only flows because ZecWallet Lite did not derive Sprout keys from the HD seed.

Can I resume a recovery scan?

Yes. ZECK stores a local recovery workspace so repeated scans can reuse synced state and recovery metadata.

What destination address should I use?

Use a Unified Address from a modern wallet that you control. Validate the address in ZECK before scanning or sweeping.
